Welcome // Residual Labs

Artist

Kristopher Huffman is a Chicago-based artist with a BS in Mathematics and an MS in Applied Probability and Statistics from Northern Illinois University. The founder of Residual Labs, his work employs mathematics, statistics, and data science as mediums for creating art and design.

His approach is deliberately low-level, with minimal reliance on external libraries. He works in R, an open-source software environment for statistical computing and graphics. His work explores how mathematical and statistical processes can be used – not just to model the world, but to create entirely new forms within it.

Cryptid Art

Residual Labs' genesis collection, Cryptid Art explores the feasibility of using stochastic processes to assemble patterns that resemble life. Explore the collection on OpenSea.
